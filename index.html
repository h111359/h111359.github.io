Summary

Hristo Hristov brings with him over 25 years of experience in the field of data and analytics. He began his career as a software engineer, delving into business information systems and Oracle database development and administration. Alongside his team, he played a key role in building the Data Warehouse for a major Bulgarian telecom operator.

Currently, Hristo leads the global data and analytics team at Coca-Cola Bulgaria. Although his current duties are focused more on leadership, talent growth, and people management, he stays connected to solution architecture and data engineering. Hristo is a Certified Microsoft Azure Solution Architect and Scrum Master.
